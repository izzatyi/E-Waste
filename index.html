ANALYSIS

Final Process

01. Initial Process

The first step involves separating plastic waste from organic waste.

02. Initial Combustion Stage

Combustion is carried out at a temperature of 350°C.

03. Advanced Combustion Stage

The combustion process is continued at a temperature of 550°C.

04. Final Combustion Stage

The combustion is conducted at a temperature of 800°C.

05. Final Results

The combustion yields steam, which is filtered to produce electricity.

BUSINESS FRAMEWORK

Our business model emphasizes innovation and sustainability by offering complementary waste management tools and collection and processing services.

Main Products

Selling large-scale equipment to factories and large institutions that generate significant plastic waste.

Waste Management Services

We provide plastic waste collection services from the community, process it into electricity at our facility, and sell the generated energy to the national electricity company or directly to customers.
